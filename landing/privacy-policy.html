Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Ufuk ("the App") may process the following information to provide prayer times:

  • Location: Used to calculate accurate prayer times. Location data is processed locally and never transmitted or stored.
  • Notification permission: Requested to send prayer time notifications. Stored on-device only.
  • Purchase information: Pro subscriptions managed via RevenueCat. All payments processed directly by Apple App Store.

2. How We Use Data

The App uses data to: calculate prayer times, send notifications, verify Pro status, and save preferences. All data is stored locally. No personal data is shared with third parties.

3. Third Party Services

  • RevenueCat: Pro subscription management.
  • Aladhan API: Online prayer times. Anonymous requests, no personal data.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Deleting the App removes all local data. Manage subscriptions via Apple Account.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Updates will be posted on this page.
