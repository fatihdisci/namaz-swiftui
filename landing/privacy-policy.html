Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Ufuk ("the App") may process the following information to provide prayer times:

  • Location: Used to calculate accurate prayer times. Location data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to or stored on our servers.
  • Notification permission: Requested to send prayer time notifications. This permission is stored on your device and not processed as personal data.
  • Purchase information: Pro subscriptions are managed through RevenueCat. All payments are processed directly by Apple App Store; the App and developer cannot access your payment information.

2. How We Use Data

The App uses data for the following purposes:

  • Calculating prayer times
  • Sending notifications
  • Verifying Pro subscription status
  • Saving in-app preferences (city, calculation method, language)

All data is stored locally on your device. The App does not share any personal data with third parties.

3. Third Party Services

  • RevenueCat: Pro subscription management. Processes purchase receipts for Apple App Store verification.
  • Aladhan API: Used for online prayer times. Anonymous requests are sent, containing no personal data.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

When you delete the App, all local data is automatically removed. To manage subscription data via RevenueCat, please visit Apple Account page.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Changes

This policy may be updated. Changes will be posted on this page.

7. Contact

For questions about this policy: fatihdisci@icloud.com
